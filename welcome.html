Welcome to Digital Women's Collections

This digital resource guide is an open-acess informational website linking researchers, educators, and students to collections specific to topics on women and their stories.

Commitment to Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

This website is a collection of digital resources that recognizes equal opportunity for every visitor. The website is my commitment to an inclusive and friendly environment that fully represents many different cultures, backgrounds, and viewpoints. My hope and vision is to inspire students, educators, and researchers to learn more about women's issues and to create a welcoming place for people from all walks of life to learn.
