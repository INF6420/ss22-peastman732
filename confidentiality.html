Student Diversity Survey - Welcome



* The survey has 22 questions and is VOLUNTARY and CONFIDENTIAL

* The survey is designed as a online instrument for the School of Information Science

* After completing the survey, click the ‘submit’ button and wait for the ‘thank you’ message before closing your browser

* If you have any questions about the survey, please contact the SIS program at sis@wayne.edu or 313-555-5555

* Completing the survey should take about 15-20 minutes

Wayne State University is committed to providing the best possible environment for all individuals within the campus community. An important step in creating a truly inclusive community is to understand students, faculty, and staff perspectives and experiences related to their work and study. This survey is one of many surveys designed to help us gain insight and understanding about our community related to diversity, equity, and inclusion. The data collected will be used to understand the present climate within the Wayne State University School of Information Sciences program and to provide support for our current student body. Your participation and responses will be strictly confidential and will not be part of any medical, employment, or disciplinary record. No individual identifiable information will be collected or reported. This survey is completely voluntary and you may EXIT at any time using the EXIT link on the navigation bar above. If you choose to participate, please remember to SUBMIT your selections at the end of the survey. To accurately determine the student diversity composition in the SIS program it is important to complete this survey only once during each academic year of your program. If you would like to proceed, please click the Begin Survey button below to begin. Thank you in advance for your time and participation!
