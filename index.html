Welcome to Digital Women's Collections

Welcome to the Digital Women's Collections. This digital resource guide is an open-acess informational website linking researchers, educators, and students to collections specific to topics on women and their stories. Please visit our In The News section to find news around the world that impacts women and women's issues. Visit our Digital Libraries page to find digital libraries around the country. Feel free to send us comments or suggestions by using our Contact page. For educators and students visit Learn More page.
